Strawberry Pie

Here it is! Our top favorite, incredibly easy, and irresistibly delicious strawberry pie, perfect for warm-weather gatherings. This pie is made for strawberry enthusiasts who want to celebrate their harvest with the best strawberry dessert.

For the best results, chill this pie overnight, making it perfect for preparing in advance. You can make the pie (without the whipped cream) up to two days ahead of time. If your fridge contains any strong odors, cover the pie tightly with plastic wrap. Add the fresh whipped cream and strawberry garnish just before serving.
